Dr. Chenglong Duan

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at Rice University in the Department of Earth, Environmental and Planetary Sciences.

Google Scholar LinkedIn ResearchGate ORCID GitHub

Research Interests

  • Connect computational seismology with observational seismology.
  • Develop exploration-scale imaging methods to study solid Earth structures (e.g., magmatic system, energy resources) and mitigate natural hazards, with potential extensions to other planets (Moon, Mars).
  • Leverage AI techniques to automatically label massive seismic data by source types (e.g., explosions, earthquakes, induced seismicity).
